Expand description

⚠️ Computes a * b * 2^(-BITS) mod modulus

⚠️ This function is not part of the stable API.
Requires that `inv` is the inverse of `-modulus[0]` modulo `2^64`. Requires that `a` and `b` are less than `modulus`.
