        -               - span

                        - ElementEnd::Empty
        --              - span

§

Text

Text token.

Contains text between elements including whitespaces. Basically everything between > and <. Except ]]>, which is not allowed and will lead to an error.

<p> text </p>
   ------     - text

The token span is equal to the text.

Fields

§text: StrSpan<'a>
§

Cdata

CDATA token.

<p><![CDATA[text]]></p>
            ----        - text
   ----------------     - span

§text: StrSpan<'a>
§span: StrSpan<'a>
