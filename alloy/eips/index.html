§alloy-eips

Ethereum Improvement Proprosal (EIP) implementations.

Contains constants, helpers, and basic data structures for consensus EIPs.

EIPs 2930 and 7702 are re-exported from alloy-rs/eips.

§Current support

  • EIP-1559
  • EIP-1898
  • EIP-2718
  • EIP-2930
